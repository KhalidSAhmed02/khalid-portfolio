Programming

My Journey

Programming wasn’t always part of my academic path, but during my third year of university (2022–2023), I began self-learning after realizing how essential it was to the modern world. Since then, I’ve been consistently building skills in Python and HTML, with a growing foundation in SQL and Java. What drives me is the desire to not only build websites for personal use but also to learn how to structure and manipulate data into clean, understandable deliverables. My larger goal is to use programming as a tool to strengthen my understanding of economics, business development, and finance, and to support my personal projects and entrepreneurial efforts.

Next Stages & Plans

  • Complete larger personal projects that reflect my skillset and creativity.
  • Develop portfolio-worthy apps to showcase my growth.
  • Continue expanding knowledge in full-stack web development and data analytics.
  • Continue to expand my experience through freelance work and personal projects, giving me practical, real-world applications.
  • Refine coding style, improve testing practices, and build scalable applications.
