Economics

My Journey

Economics has been both a passion and a formal academic focus, culminating in my Bachelor’s Honors degree. My interest started back in high school when I began to grasp how deeply the economy affects everyday life, not just on paper, but in the realities of communities, families, and individual well-being. Through my degree, I explored areas like labor economics, international trade, business data analysis, and health economics, each of which shaped my thinking on how economic forces intersect with people’s lives. My ambition is to apply this knowledge in community-facing roles, especially in economic development for cities, towns, or regions. Every day, I work to move closer to a career where I can help foster sustainable economic growth that benefits individuals and the collective community.

Next Stages & Plans

  • Continue improving and updating my research papers on wealth distribution, expanding the depth of analysis using real-world sample data.
  • Explore new areas of study within economics to broaden both theoretical understanding and practical knowledge.
  • Find platforms or spaces where I can share my ideas, analyses, and perspectives with others.
  • Seek opportunities to contribute to economic projects that directly impact communities or organizations.
