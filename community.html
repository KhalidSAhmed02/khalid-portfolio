Community

My Journey

Community has always been at the center of what I believe in. I strongly feel that the erosion of community togetherness lies at the heart of many modern social and societal challenges. Throughout my life, I’ve been active in giving back, from volunteering at Somali Relief to supporting local charities and my long-term dream is to work in economic roles that are community-based, where I can help foster entrepreneurship, empower individuals, and create tangible, long-lasting impact. I believe deeply that resilient, tight-knit communities are the foundation for meaningful progress.

Next Stages & Plans

  • Continue researching and writing about community issues, particularly those affecting marginalized or underserved populations.
  • Deepen my understanding of how historical and modern changes have led to the weakening of communities over the last few centuries.
  • Pursue roles that combine economics and policy to help communities grow stronger, fairer, and more connected.
  • Commit to learning by doing, contributing directly through volunteering and project-based community work.
